# Visual Cards

This folder contains selected public visual cards from Livingtelligence.

The cards are designed to make human-AI phenomena easier to recognize, discuss, and teach.

They are not the full phenomenological canon. They are sample public artifacts.

## Purpose

Visual cards help teams:

- recognize patterns that may otherwise remain invisible
- introduce shared language
- support education and awareness
- connect phenomena to mitigation and nourishment practices
- make abstract human-AI dynamics more discussable

## Public Scope

These examples are selected for public communication.

They do not include the full internal canon, source registries, client materials, or private stewardship assets.

## Livingtelligence Use

In Livingtelligence, visual cards are part of a broader asset layer.

Assets help translate observation into:

- education
- prevention
- mitigation
- nourishment
- stewardship
- timely intervention
