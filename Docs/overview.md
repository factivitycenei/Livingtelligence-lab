# Livingtelligence Overview

Livingtelligence is an applied research, methodology, and prototype initiative for governing co-intelligence in organizations.

It helps organizations observe, understand, prevent, mitigate, and nourish the human and institutional effects that emerge when AI becomes part of everyday work.

## The Basic Problem

AI is no longer only a tool people consult.

It increasingly participates in:

- framing questions
- generating options
- drafting judgments
- accelerating outputs
- shaping confidence
- coordinating tasks
- preserving or distorting context

The surface risk is that AI may make mistakes.

The deeper risk is that organizations may lose the ability to see which human capacities are being strengthened, weakened, displaced, or transformed.

## What Livingtelligence Does

Livingtelligence helps organizations make human-AI phenomena observable and governable.

It combines:

- a phenomenological canon of human-AI patterns
- a Signal Readout process for early diagnosis
- stewardship practices for internal capacity-building
- mitigation and nourishment assets
- visual and pedagogical materials
- a lightweight Custodian architecture for observation, memory, and follow-up

## More Than Observation

Livingtelligence does not only observe phenomena.

It helps organizations:

- recognize emerging patterns
- educate teams before patterns normalize
- mitigate harmful dynamics
- nourish capacities such as judgment, agency, learning, deliberation, dignity, and institutional coherence
- intervene through stewards, protocols, workshops, visual cards, diagnostics, and governance practices

## The Three-Agent Context

Livingtelligence works in a probabilistic organizational context where three forms of agency increasingly co-act:

- **Human agency**: judgment, authorship, responsibility, care, deliberation, and correction.
- **Artificial agency**: delegated digital capacity for analysis, synthesis, coordination, generation, memory, and action.
- **Organizational agency**: the structures, norms, roles, incentives, memory, and accountability systems that govern how people and AI systems interact.

The central question is not only whether AI performs well.

It is whether the organization can preserve and nourish human goods while intelligence is redistributed across these three agents.

## Working Formula

```text
Signals -> Phenomena -> Readouts -> Stewards -> Assets -> Prevention / Mitigation / Nourishment / Correction
```
